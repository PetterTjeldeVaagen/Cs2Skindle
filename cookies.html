Cookie Information

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to collect information about how you use our site. This helps us to improve our website and provide a better service to you.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons, including:

  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To understand how you use our website and to improve its performance.
  • To provide you with relevant content and advertisements.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data such as the number of visitors to the site, where visitors have come to the site from, and the pages they visited. This information helps us to improve our website and provide a better user experience.

For more information on how Google Analytics uses cookies, please visit Google's Cookie Policy.

Managing cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. For more information, please visit AboutCookies.org. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.
